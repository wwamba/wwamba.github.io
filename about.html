About

Warren has 5 years of experience as a consulting engineer specializing in the delivery of strategic pieces of work for market and counterparty risk departments of the world's main investment banks both in Paris and London.

An experience at a Big Four company as a Senior Financial Risk Management Quantitative Advisor in London allowed him to work on the identification, quantification and mitigation of model risk mainly.

During his career as a consulting engineer, he developed qualities of rigor, team-spirit and initiative as well as solid analytical skills.

Also, on the one hand, his knowledge of risk measures (VaR, ES, EAD, EEPE, CVA, etc.), derivatives (rates, stocks, etc.) and associated pricing and, on the other hand, his mastery of main development languages allows him to be a proposal strength for the teams with which he collaborates.

In a nutshell, he has a specialist level expertise in modelling and banking risks along with a deep knowledge of the regulatory initiatives re-shaping the financial industry since 2008.
