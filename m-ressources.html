The Credit Default Swap Life Cycle

Quantitative Studies Vol 1, No. 7, Oct. 2017

This video retraces the life cycle of an insurance contract against the default of a counterparty better known under the name of Credit Default Swap (CDS).


From a little historical reminder, we arrive at the auction process which occurs when a company on which these contracts exist default.


This video is largely inspired by the article of the same name produced by the american business bank Morgan Stanley and is the result of an ESILV engineering school's advance fixed income with Bloomberg course project.
