Vanilla Equity Options and Greeks Revisited

Quantitative Studies Vol 1, No. 7, Oct. 2017


Abstract


Today many equity derivatives are traded on organized and over-the-counter markets. The models that allow market participants to value them and manage the associated risks daily are numerous. The idea of this study is, for vanilla equity options, to understand the Black and Scholes model which is the most commonly used model in the matter and confront it with reality. We will, therefore, begin by recalling known results and then try to raise the problems that are encountered when attempting to apply them in a practical context.
