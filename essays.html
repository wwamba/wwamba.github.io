VBA Development: How to proceed the right way

by Warren Wamba - Last Updated on March 14, 2019


One of the strengths of VBA development is to be able to create applications and/or automation on all Office products and the interconnection if necessary in order to enhance the process of decision/analysis or to reduce the burden of repetitive tasks.


When you start a development, whatever the language used, you tend to jump straight into the code where, in reality, it is necessary to apply several principles in order to deliver a high quality application.


In this essay, we are openly discussing the frequent challenges encountered in defining the need and in designing the VBA solution.
